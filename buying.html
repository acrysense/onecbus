Home tour

Book a tour online and your Yoreevo agent will coordinate and accompany you to the showing

  • Find a property you'd like to see in person? Book a private showing online and your Yoreevo agent will coordinate and accompany you. You can also add open houses to your schedule.
  • Yoreevo's platform will help you stay organized by keeping track of all past and future visits.
  • Your Yoreevo agent is always an email/call/text away. Take advantage of that!

Offer

Work with your agent to put together the perfect offer

  • Create the basics for an offer with a few clicks of your mouse
  • Your Yoreevo agent will go over the property's comps and offer details with you.
  • They've seen it all so feel free to ask questions!

Closing and rebate

Your Yoreevo agent will walk you through the closing process and timing.

  • We will help put together deadlines, arrange for appraisal visits, walkthroughs/inspections, scheduling…etc.
  • As soon as we reach an accepted offer, we'll get started familiarizing you with and organizing the board application.
  • Our goal is to make the process as smooth as possible and of course, get you a big commission rebate!
